<h1>PLANT (Unity)</h1>

 ### [Video and Details](https://www.camfaulkner.dev/plant)
 
<h2>Description</h2>

PLANT is a 3d adventure puzzle game made in Unity, and developed as part of one of my final year University modules. In this singleplayer game, you are a flower who is tasked with cleansing the large world around you of a decay that has been created by caterpillars.
